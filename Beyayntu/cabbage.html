Cabbage Dish

One 9-inch pie

30 Min - Prep time

10 Min - Cook time

40 Min - Total

Servings

INGREDIENTS

  • ½ cup olive oil
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 4 carrot, (7-1/2")s carrots, thinly sliced
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 teaspoon sea salt
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • ½ teaspoon ground cumin
  • ¼ teaspoon ground turmeric
  • ½ head cabbage, shredded
  • 5 eaches potatoes, peeled and cut into 1-inch cubes

DIRECTIONS

  1. Preheat pan: Preheat pan .

    2. Heat the olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Cook the carrots and onion in the hot oil about 5 minutes. Stir in the salt, pepper, cumin, turmeric, and cabbage and cook another 15 to 20 minutes. Add the potatoes; cover. Reduce heat to medium-low and cook until potatoes are soft, 20 to 30 minutes.
